# Cookie Policy

*Last updated: May 2, 2026*

## 1. Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how xScaler Labs uses cookies and similar technologies on our documentation site and management portal.

---

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They are widely used to make websites function correctly and to provide usage information to site owners.

---

## 3. Cookies We Use

| Type | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Essential** | Required for the portal and documentation site to function (e.g., session management, authentication state). |
| **Analytics** | Used to understand how visitors interact with our documentation, so we can improve it. |
| **Preferences** | Remember settings such as colour mode (light/dark). |

---

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

Our documentation site is built with Docusaurus and may include cookies set by third-party services (e.g., analytics providers). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

---

## 5. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the portal.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check back periodically for any changes.
